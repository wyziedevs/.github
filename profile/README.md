# Wyzie Developers
Providing the world with free subtitles. We depend on the teamwork and collaboration from others to build our projects, we promote feedback and discussion in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/2mxraHBVtB).

## Our Promises
* No censorship or filtering of media
* We build for ourselves and others
* We are open source and transparent about our work
* We are not a company, we are a team of volunteers
